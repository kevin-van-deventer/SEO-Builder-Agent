# On-Page & Content Optimization Playbook

Guidelines for search-engine-optimized heading architectures, keyword mapping, and copywriting styles.

## 1. Structured Writing Priority Checklist
- **Page Title**: < 60 characters, with primary keyword near the start: `"Keyword | Brand Name"`.
- **Meta Description**: < 160 characters, natural keyword integration, compelling call-to-action (CTA).
- **Heading Hierarchy Law**:
  - Exactly **one** `<h1>` per page containing the primary keyword.
  - Subheadings (`<h2>`, `<h3>`) must nest sequentially without level skipping (e.g. no `<h1>` → `<h3>` transitions).
- **First 100 Words**: Naturally include the primary keyword in the first paragraph of copy.
- **Copy Length Target**:
  - Homepage: 500 - 1,000 words
  - Service Page: 800 - 1,500 words
  - Blog Post: 1,500 - 3,000 words

---

## 2. Dynamic Keyword Hierarchy Maps

Ensure target keywords are mapped strictly according to this priority hierarchy:

```
[Title Tag] ➔ [H1 Tag] ➔ [First 100 Words] ➔ [URL Slug] ➔ [Meta Description] ➔ [H2/H3 Tags]
```

---

## 3. Local SEO Integration
For physical businesses, Nap variables (Name, Address, Phone) must be consistent in the footer, contact page, and location-targeted service outlines. Embed active, secure Google Maps elements on contact layouts.
